ABOUT

RwisiiDriss, known as Idriss RS on YouTube, is a 17-year-old creator living in Tunisia with a natural spark for creativity. Since he was seven, he has loved playing piano and singing, letting music shape his emotions and imagination. From a young age, he turned simple daily moments into stories full of rhythm and feeling. Everyone around him knew he was destined to create something special.

As a vlogger, Idriss RS captures his life with honesty, cinematic detail, and a unique Tunisian vibe. The streets he walks, the sunsets he films, and the adventures with friends all become part of his visual diary. Cinema isn’t just a hobby for him — it’s the language he speaks best. Through every cut, every melody, and every shot, he expresses the world the way he sees it.

Despite his young age, he has collaborated with many restaurants, clubs, and local brands who trust his vision. He brings authenticity, emotion, and youthful energy to every project he touches. Balancing school, music, filmmaking, and vlogging, he inspires others with his passion and dedication. For him, this is only the beginning — the first chapter of a storyteller meant to go far.
